## What this app is
PocketPoker is a location-based social app for discovering and organizing
private poker games. It provides coordination, reputation, and discovery tools.

## What this app is NOT
- Not an online poker platform
- Not a gambling or wagering service
- Does not process payments or handle money
- Does not track winnings or enforce buy-ins

## Core principles
- Trust and safety first
- Host-controlled access
- Information, not facilitation
- Address privacy by default

## Legal constraints (non-negotiable)
- No in-app payments
- Buy-ins are informational only
- 21+ age gate
- App does not organize games

## Target MVP features
- User profiles with reputation
- Host-created rooms
- Join request + approval workflow
- Location-based discovery (PostGIS)
- Room chat

## Explicit non-goals
- No escrow
- No rake
- No tournaments
- No gameplay logic
